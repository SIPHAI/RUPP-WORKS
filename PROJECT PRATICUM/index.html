Royal University of Phnom Penh

The Royal University of Phnom Penh is the oldest largest public university in Cambodia it was founded un 1960 and is located in the capital city of Phnom Penh. RUPP is a leading Institute of higher education in Cambodia. with a strong reputation for academic excellence and search. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as social sciences, natural sciences, engineering, education, law,and humanities. RUPP has several research centers and institutes that focus on various areas of study, including the Institute of Technology of Cambodia, the Institute of Foreign Languages,and the Center for Khmer Studies. With a student population of over 20.00, RUPP is committed to providing quality education and renowned institutions around the word. RUPP is university has a highly qualified faculty with many professors holding advanced degrees from renowned institutions around the world. RUPP is actively involved in promoting community development and social responsibility through carious initiatives and programs that support sustainable development, environment conservation, ans social justice in Cambodia and beyond. Overall,the Royal University of Phnom Penh is a premier institution of higher learning in Cambodia, providing students with a top-quality education, a diverse range of academic programs,and opportunities for research and community engagement.
